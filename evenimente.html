Events

Discover the latest events at our museum, including workshops, lectures, and special exhibitions. Stay updated on our calendar to make sure you don't miss out on any exciting opportunities to engage with art and culture.

Art Workshop: Exploring Creativity

Join us for an interactive art workshop where you can unleash your creativity and learn new techniques from our experienced artists. This event is suitable for all skill levels, so whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist, there's something for everyone.

Lecture Series: The History of Art

Attend our lecture series where renowned art historians will delve into the fascinating history of art. From ancient civilizations to contemporary movements, these lectures will provide insights into the evolution of artistic expression and its impact on society.

Family Day: Art Activities for All Ages

Bring the whole family for a day of fun and creativity! Our Family Day event offers a variety of art activities and workshops designed for all ages. Enjoy hands-on experiences, interactive exhibits, and special performances that will inspire creativity in everyone.
